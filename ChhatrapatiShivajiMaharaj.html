Life

  • Early Life: Shivaji was born in 1630 at Shivneri Fort in Pune, Maharashtra. His father, Shahaji Bhonsle, was a Maratha general, and his mother, Jijabai, played a significant role in shaping his character.
  • Coronation:Shivaji was crowned as the Chhatrapati (monarch) of the Maratha Kingdom in 1674, adopting the title "Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj." The coronation took place at the Raigad Fort.
